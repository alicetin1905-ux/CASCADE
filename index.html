Contrarian model: a one-sided liquidation cascade + volume spike + stretched RSI on a closed candle opens the fade. Longs liquidated → look long; shorts liquidated → look short. Fills, TP/SL and fees are simulated on candle range. Nothing is sent to any exchange. Balance, trades, veto counts and the observed-liquidation dataset persist in this browser per symbol/timeframe; "reset balance" and "apply & reset" clear the account but never the liquidation dataset. Open positions are not persisted — reloads start flat.
